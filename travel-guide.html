Essential Holiday Checklist

Entry & Getting Around

Before you fly to Malaysia, make sure you have all your travel documents in order to ensure a smooth transition.

  1. Passport Validity: Passports must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your departure date.
  2. Digital Arrival Card (MDAC): All foreign citizens must submit the Malaysia Digital Arrival Card online within 3 days prior to arrival.
  3. Ride-Hailing Apps: Download "Grab" on your smartphone. It is the cheapest and safest ride-hailing transport option in Malaysian cities.
  4. SIM Card: Purchase a local tourist eSIM or physical SIM (Maxis/Celcomdigi) at the airport for cheap data.

What to Pack

Malaysia's tropical weather is hot and humid. Dress comfortably and pack smart for nature activities.

  • Lightweight, breathable cotton clothing.
  • A compact travel umbrella or lightweight raincoat for sudden rain.
  • Comfortable walking shoes for exploring heritage streets and national parks.
  • Modest clothing (covering shoulders and knees) for temple and mosque visits.
  • High SPF sunscreen and insect repellent with DEET.
